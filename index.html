Welcome to Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. List of Characters that appear in the Jujutsu Kaisen series.

Yuji Itadori
Yuji Itadori is a fictional character and the protagonist of the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen created by Gege Akutami.
Ryomen Sukuna
Ryomen Sukuna is a fictional character and one of the main antagonists of the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. A sorcerer from the Heian Era, he was known as the "King of Curses" and considered the most powerful of all time.
Satoru Gojo
Satoru Gojo is a fictional character from the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen created by Gege Akutami. He made his first appearance in the prequel anime Jujutsu Kaisen 0 as the master of Yuta Okkotsu, a cursed teenager.
